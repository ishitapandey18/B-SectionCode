Tribute to KK

A voice that lives forever in our hearts

About KK

Krishnakumar Kunnath, popularly known as KK, was a celebrated Indian playback singer who touched millions of hearts with his soulful voice. Known for timeless hits like "Tadap Tadap", "Yaaron", "Pyaar Ke Pal", and "Zara Sa", KK's songs defined an era of emotions, nostalgia, and musical brilliance.

KK's versatility across languages and genres made him a legend.

Legacy

Though he left us too soon in 2022, KK's voice continues to inspire and heal. He may be gone from the stage, but he lives on in every beat, every note, every heart that feels his music.
